Learn How to Delete SpendRep's Backup Data and Revoke its Google Drive Access

Detailed guide for managing SpendRep's backup, connection, and account deletion settings.

1. Delete SpendRep's Backup Data Only (Keep Connection Active)

Use this option if you want to delete **only SpendRep's backup data** stored on Google Drive, but wish to keep the connection active for future backups.

  1. Step 1: Open the **Settings** menu in your application.
  2. Step 2: Select **"Backup data to Google Drive"**.
  3. Step 3: Click the **"Delete Backup Data"** button.
  4. Note: Your SpendRep account and local data will remain active.
  5. Warning: This action will permanently delete your SpendRep's backup data on Google Drive.

2. Delete SpendRep's Backup Data and Disconnect Its Google Drive Access

This action will **delete all SpendRep's backup data** *and* **disconnect its access** to your Google Drive, preventing future backups.

  1. Step 1: Open the **Settings** menu in your application.
  2. Step 2: Select **"Backup data to Google Drive"**.
  3. Step 3: Click the **"Delete Backup Data and Disconnect from Google Drive"** button.
  4. Warning: This action is permanent. It will permanently delete your SpendRep's backup data on Google Drive and disconnect its access.

3. Delete Your SpendRep Local Data and Profile (Full Deletion)

**SpendRep is a serverless application and does not store your account or transaction data on our servers.** All your financial data is stored locally on your device or in your personal Google Drive (if backed up).

Steps for Full Deletion:

  1. Step A: **Delete Cloud Backup Data**

    Follow the steps in **Section 2** above to delete SpendRep's backup data and disconnect its Google Drive access.

  2. Step B: **Clear Local Data and Profile**

    Go to your device's operating system settings and manually clear the app's storage to erase all local transactions and profile data:

    • **Android:** Settings > Apps > SpendRep > Storage & cache > **Clear storage** (or **Clear Data**).

Data Deletion Scope (Upon completing Steps A & B):

  • **Deleted:** All user-provided financial transaction data, custom categories, user settings, and unique user identifiers stored locally on the device (via Shared Preferences or local database).
  • **Deleted:** All associated SpendRep's backup data on Google Drive (from Step A).
  • **Retained:** No personal or financial data is retained by the developer (SpendRep).

If you require support or have questions about this process, you may email us at: support@spendrep.com.

Ensure you have backed up any important data locally before performing a permanent deletion or disconnection.
